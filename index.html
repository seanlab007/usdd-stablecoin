The Peg Mechanism: Dual-Commodity Backing

Electricity Price Anchor

According to Dark Matter Bank, 1 USDD can be used to purchase electricity at the daily grid price published by China's NDRC. The issuer has stated strategic cooperation agreements with the State Grid Corporation of China and China Southern Power Grid — the world's two largest electricity transmission networks by grid scale.

Rare Earth Reserve Backing

Dark Matter Bank states that USDD is backed by China's national rare earth mineral reserves, through a strategic cooperation agreement with China Rare Earth Group Co., Ltd. (中国稀土集团有限公司), the national entity established by the Chinese government in 2021. Institutional holders of USDD receive preferential purchasing rights for all 17 rare earth elements.

China controls approximately 85% of global rare earth reserves and over 60% of global rare earth production capacity. The 17 elements covered include lanthanum, cerium, neodymium, dysprosium, and others critical for electric vehicles, wind turbines, defense electronics, and semiconductor manufacturing.
